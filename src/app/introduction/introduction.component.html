About me

Naturally curious and driven, I’m someone who thrives on complex challenges — I’m always eager to learn, go deeper, and push boundaries. Whether it’s mastering a new concept or untangling a tough technical issue, I bring energy and persistence to every project.

Fluent in both code and collaboration, I strive to bridge business needs and technical execution through pragmatic, scalable solutions. I'm also comfortable operating in DevOps-ready environments using Docker and Kubernetes.
